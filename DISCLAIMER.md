demonstrate production-style software architecture, Firebase governance,
CDSS-style rule explanation, data provenance, and release operations.

ParkinSUM Companion is not a medical device, is not clinical software, and does
not provide medical advice. It must not be used for diagnosis, treatment,
medication timing, dietary decisions, patient care, emergency decisions, or
personal health management.

Public demonstrations must use synthetic or sample data only. Do not enter real
health information, medication schedules, symptoms, identifiers, Firebase
tokens, service account credentials, user exports, or raw operator audit logs.

No external clinical, legal, privacy, regulatory, or professional review is
claimed by this public repository. Any future real-world health or clinical use
would require a separate intended-use statement, professional review, privacy
