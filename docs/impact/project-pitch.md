# Project Pitch

## Short Pitch

ParkinSUM Companion is an educational Flutter prototype that demonstrates how a
local-first app can help explain Parkinson's disease diet-medication awareness
with deterministic rules, synthetic demo data, and evidence-oriented safety
copy.

It is not medical advice and does not make personal treatment decisions.

## 30-Second Version

ParkinSUM explores a responsible pattern for digital-health education. A visitor
can enter synthetic meal and medication context, run a deterministic
food-drug-awareness check, and see an explanation that points back to rule logic
and evidence context. The project avoids real patient data and intentionally
limits claims, so it can be discussed safely in GitHub, school, mentoring,
research-portfolio, and community presentation settings.

## Presentation Version

Many health-technology demos are impressive but hard to trust because it is not
clear where the result came from. ParkinSUM takes the opposite approach. It uses
a Flutter app, local-first data flow, deterministic rules, and synthetic demo
scenarios to make the reasoning inspectable.

The prototype focuses on Parkinson's disease diet-medication awareness as an
educational topic. It does not tell people how to take medication or what to eat.
Instead, it shows how structured inputs, rule triggers, evidence references, and
safety language can be combined in a public-facing app without collecting real
patient data.

The impact is mainly educational and technical: ParkinSUM gives teachers,
mentors, reviewers, and open-source visitors a concrete example of responsible
digital-health prototyping.

## Audience Fit

- GitHub visitors: inspect the Flutter app, tests, CI, and public docs.
- Teachers and mentors: use it to discuss responsible software design.
- School or scholarship reviewers: review the technical architecture and safety
  framing.
- Digital-health audiences: discuss local-first data handling, deterministic
  rules, and careful claims.
- Research portfolio readers: evaluate it as a prototype and communication
  artifact, not a clinical study.

## What To Emphasize

- Educational prototype.
- Synthetic/demo data only.
- Local-first public demo flow.
- Deterministic rule engine instead of opaque conflict decisions.
- Evidence-oriented explanations.
- Conservative safety and ethics boundary.

## What Not To Claim

- Do not claim diagnosis, treatment, monitoring, prevention, or medication
  management.
- Do not claim patient outcome improvement.
- Do not claim regulatory approval or medical-device status.
- Do not imply that the app has completed clinical validation.
- Do not present examples as real patient cases.

## Suggested Closing Line

ParkinSUM is a responsible software prototype: it makes the app logic visible,
keeps examples synthetic, and uses conservative language so digital-health ideas
can be demonstrated without overstating medical claims.
