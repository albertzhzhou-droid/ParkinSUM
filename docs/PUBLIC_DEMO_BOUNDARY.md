# Public Demo Boundary

The public demo boundary exists to make ParkinSUM visible as a software
architecture prototype without turning it into a real health tool.

## Allowed

- Synthetic sample cases.
- Catalog and rule-explanation walkthroughs.
- Firebase governance and Firestore rules review.
- Provenance and release evidence review.
- Local development and test runs.

## Not Allowed

- Real health, medication, symptom, meal, or patient records.
- Public registration for health use.
- Real-world health decisions.
- Claims of clinical validation, legal approval, privacy certification, or
  regulatory clearance.
- Uploading local token files, user exports, raw audit logs, or service account
  credentials.

## Demo Text Standard

Use these labels when presenting the project publicly:

- `Production-architecture prototype`
- `Educational research prototype`
- `Synthetic-data demo`
- `CDSS-style rule explanation`

Avoid labels that imply real-world clinical use.
